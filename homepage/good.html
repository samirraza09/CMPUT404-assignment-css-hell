Zachary "Sneaky" Scuderi

Zachary "Sneaky" Scuderi is a former professional League of Legends player for Cloud9. He played the bot lane position, and for the duration of his 6 year tenure with C9 he was considered a top 3 AD Carry in the LCS. While Sneaky was a pro player he also had a very popular Twitch stream. He mostly streams his League of Legends gameplay but also plays other games. Since he retired as a professional player he has began co-streaming LCS games with former teammate William "Meteos" Hartman and former AD Carry rival Yilliang "Doublelift" Peng.

Professional History

Mid Laner for Oridnance Gaming, April - June 2012
AD Carry for Absolute Legends, June - September 2012
AD Carry for Pulse Esports, September 2012 - February 2013
AD Carry for Dignitas, February - April 2013
AD Carry for Quantic Gaming, April - May 2013
AD Carry for Cloud 9, May 2013 - January 2020
